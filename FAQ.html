skBOMA Firepits.

At skBoma Firepits, we specialize in crafting durable, portable, and stylish fire solutions that enhance any space or adventure. Inspired by the essence of social gatherings and the beauty of the outdoors, our firepits are built with precision and care using high-quality carbon steel.

Designed by Sebastian Kirkland, a renowned industrial designer and Co-founder of User Design International, these firepits reflect a perfect blend of innovation and craftsmanship. With over 25 years of expertise in design and manufacturing, Sebastian has collaborated with some of South Africa's leading interior and architectural design houses. His achievements include winning the 2010 Design Indaba Best Product Award and securing a major contract with SAB as a Creative Director for Point of Sale. Additionally, his designs have been internationally patented, showcasing his commitment to excellence and innovation.

This larger-than-most fire pit on the market has the unique ability to flat pack for mobility while still remaining aesthetically pure to form, making it ideal for camping, braais, or relaxing evenings under the stars. Whether you're creating warmth at home or on the go, skBOMA Firepits deliver functionality, durability, and timeless style to every occasion.
